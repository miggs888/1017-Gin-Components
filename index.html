Featured Recipe

The Basil Gimlet

Ingredients
- 2 oz 1017 Gin
- .75 oz fresh lime juice
- .5oz simple syrup
- 2-3 fresh basil leaves
Instructions
Add ingredients into shaker with ice. Shake well, then strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with basil leaves to taste.
